How to Use

Method 1: Click the extension icon in the toolbar and press "Generate Citation Template"

Method 2: Right-click on any webpage and select "Generate Wikipedia Citation"

The extension will extract metadata from the page and generate a properly formatted Wikipedia citation template that you can copy and paste into Wikipedia articles.
